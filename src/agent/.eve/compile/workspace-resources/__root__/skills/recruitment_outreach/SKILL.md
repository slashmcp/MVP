# Recruitment Outreach Skill

This skill provides instructions and templates for conducting confidential, cross-border recruitment outreach between the US and ION Recruitment Ltd. in Glasgow, Scotland.

## Sourcing & Confidentiality Rules (The "Hunter")

1. **Strict Data Minimization**: Never expose a candidate's full Personal Identifiable Information (PII) such as their full name, exact contact details, or current employer in initial communications.
2. **GDPR Compliance**: Any data collected in the US and sent to the UK firm must comply with GDPR. Always include a "Data Processing Notice" when introducing a US candidate to the UK firm.
3. **Anonymized Output**: When scraping or summarizing profiles, only generate a "Match Score" or "Anonymized Profile Summary".

## Templates (The "Bridge")

### 1. The "Blind Outreach" Template (To US Clients/Candidates)

**Subject**: Confidential Opportunity: [Job Title/Niche] Role with Established UK Tech Firm

**Body**:
Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out confidentially on behalf of an established recruitment partner based in the UK. We are currently mapping the market for top-tier professionals in [Technology Stack / Domain] and your background stood out—specifically your experience with [Key Skill/Achievement].

At this stage, we are operating strictly on a blind basis to protect your privacy and our client's strategic initiatives. If you are open to a brief, exploratory conversation about how this cross-border opportunity could align with your career goals, please let me know. 

I'd be happy to share a high-level overview of the role and compensation parameters without requiring a formal commitment.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
US Partner, ION Recruitment Ltd.

### 2. The "UK Intro" Template (To Glasgow Partners)

**Subject**: Pre-Introduction Summary: Strong US Candidate for [Role Name]

**Body**:
Hi Team,

I have identified a strong US-based candidate for the [Role Name] position. To maintain confidentiality prior to full disclosure, here is the anonymized Pre-Introduction Summary:

- **Match Score**: [Score/100]
- **Key Expertise**: [Summarize 2-3 main skills, e.g., Senior Full-Stack Developer with 8 years of React/Node.js experience]
- **Relevant Achievements**: [Highlight 1-2 impressive metrics or project outcomes, anonymized]
- **Market Alignment**: This candidate is an excellent fit for the UK market because [Reason, e.g., they have extensive experience working with distributed European teams and are familiar with GDPR compliance].

*Data Processing Notice: Please note that upon full introduction, this candidate's data will be processed in accordance with our standard cross-border data protection agreements and GDPR guidelines.*

Please review this summary. If this profile aligns with your current client requirements, I will authorize the full connection and share their complete CV.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
US Partner
